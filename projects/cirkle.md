## What it does

Cirkle was a mobile application designed to help post-secondary students stay engaged and connected, addressing root causes of student dropout in higher education.

## Why I built it

After researching the leading causes of student dropout, my co-founders and I identified a gap in how students build community and support networks at university. We built Cirkle to close that gap.

## Technical challenges

- Built and published a full-stack mobile app from scratch with a small team
- Coordinated with software developers on timelines and feature goals while managing investor expectations
- Designed the front-end and contributed to backend development using React JS and AWS cloud integration
- Conducted 200+ user studies applying HCI principles to iteratively refine the interface

## Current status

Archived. The company ran from January to October 2021. The app was published on the Google Play Store during that period.
